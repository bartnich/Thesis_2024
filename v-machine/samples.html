All texts are encoded according to TEI's Parallel Segmentation method or the Location-referenced method, two feature of the TEI Guidelines that allow multiple versions of a text to be encoded in one document or apparatus. The Versioning Machine interprets the encoding, parsing out the text into its constituent parts.
